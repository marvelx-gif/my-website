Kodex (KDX)

Kodex (KDX) is an emerging payment-focused cryptocurrency built on Solana, with its roots firmly planted in Kenya. Revolutionizing digital transactions with speed, low cost, and inclusivity.

Mission-Driven Approach

Kodex bridges financial gaps in Africa where remittance fees are high and banking access is limited. It empowers merchants, freelancers, and individuals with fast, low-cost crypto tools.

Why Solana?

Solana provides thousands of TPS at ultra-low fees. Perfect for real-world payments, eco-friendly, scalable, and beginner-friendly with Phantom integration.
